ConferenceCaptioning Business Plan

Revolutionizing event accessibility with AI-powered live captions and translations.

Executive Summary

ConferenceCaptioning is revolutionizing event accessibility by providing a simple, reliable solution for live captions and translations. Our iPhone and Mac app empowers event organizers and AV companies to make their events inclusive for the deaf, hard of hearing, and non-native speakers with the tap of a button. By scanning a QR code, attendees can access live captions and translations directly on their devices—no downloads, no hassle.

The Problem

Despite growing diversity and accessibility requirements, many live events remain inaccessible to audiences with hearing impairments or language barriers. Current solutions are often cumbersome, costly, or fail to provide real-time multilingual capabilities.

Our Solution

ConferenceCaptioning combines cutting-edge AI and Natural Language Processing to transcribe speech in real-time and translate it into over 20 languages without requiring an internet connection. This ensures privacy, reliability, and seamless usability, even in challenging environments.

Market Analysis

Industry Overview

The global events industry is projected to grow to $2.33 trillion by 2026, driven by increasing demand for in-person and hybrid events. Accessibility and inclusivity are becoming critical as organizations strive to comply with accessibility laws like the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and to address the diverse needs of global audiences. However, many live events remain inaccessible to individuals with hearing impairments or those who do not speak the primary language of the event.

In parallel, the AI-driven captioning and translation market is experiencing rapid growth, fueled by advancements in natural language processing (NLP) and real-time speech recognition technologies. This convergence presents a unique opportunity for ConferenceCaptioning to lead in accessibility innovation for live events.

Target Market Segments

Event Organizers

Corporate conferences, trade shows, and industry events.

Pain points: Need for inclusive and scalable solutions to meet accessibility compliance and audience expectations.

Audio-Visual (AV) Companies

Providers of technical services for live events.

Pain points: Lack of integrated and easy-to-use captioning/translation tools to offer to clients.

Universities and Educational Institutions

Academic conferences, lectures, and large seminars.

Pain points: Ensuring accessibility for students, faculty, and attendees from diverse backgrounds.

Government and Nonprofits

Community meetings, town halls, and global summits.

Pain points: Increasing focus on accessibility for public and diverse audiences.

Hybrid and Virtual Events

Hybrid models have gained traction post-COVID-19, amplifying the need for seamless captioning and translation solutions for global audiences.

Industry Trends

  • Increased Focus on Accessibility Compliance:

    Laws like the ADA and Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are driving demand for inclusive solutions.

  • Rising Global Audiences for Events:

    Hybrid events have brought more international attendees, increasing the need for multilingual support.

  • Technology Adoption:

    Event planners are increasingly seeking AI-powered tools that are easy to implement and scalable for large audiences.

Competitor Analysis

  • Google Live Transcribe: Effective for personal use but lacks event-specific integration and multilingual support.

Indirect Competitors:

AV companies using manual captioning solutions or contracting third-party interpreters.

ConferenceCaptioning’s Advantage:

  • Ease of use: No app downloads required for attendees; live captions and translations are accessible via a simple QR code.
  • Cost efficiency: Affordable compared to hiring interpreters or purchasing expensive AV setups.
