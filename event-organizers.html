The 3-Tap Setup

Inclusion shouldn't require hiring extra staff or a complicated AV setup.

1. Plug in your audio

Connect directly to any existing soundboard or microphone via standard inputs.

2. Open the app

Launch the app. It's pre-configured and ready to transcribe within seconds.

Meeting Accessibility Standards

Maintaining compliance isn't just about avoiding legal risks under the ADA, Section 508, and the EAA-it's about demonstrating your commitment to inclusivity. Providing accurate, reliable captions drastically improves attendee retention and boosts your event's overall inclusivity scores.

The No-Internet-Required Advantage

A Reliability Guarantee: Venue Wi-Fi is notoriously unreliable when thousands of attendees hit the network simultaneously. While other cloud-based services buffer, drop words, or stop completely during internet lags, our on-device hardware runs locally. Uninterrupted captions, 100% of the time.

Instant Transcripts

Extend the life of your event instantly. Provide your attendees, marketing team, and journalists with a full text record of every session the moment the speaker steps off stage. No more waiting days for an expensive transcriber.
