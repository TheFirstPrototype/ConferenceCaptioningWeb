Key Findings

  • Conference Captioning preserved sentence continuity across long emotional speech sequences with minimal drift.
  • The transcription remained highly readable and semantically stable throughout the evaluated sample.
  • Numerical compression such as “1 cm” improved live readability while maintaining meaning.
  • Punctuation and phrase segmentation significantly improved accessibility and cognitive readability during live playback.
  • The system demonstrated production-grade multilingual live ASR capabilities suitable for conferences and live events.
